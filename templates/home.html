Job Trace

Tracing your steps.

Job Trace allows you to visualize, share, and keep record of your job search in an aesthetic and accessible manner. When you send an application, simply make note of it in Job Trace, and update its status whenever you can. Graphs and charts will automatically create themselves and update in real time, giving you a visual representation of your journey towards your next job.

Created by David Choi

Hi, I'm David, I'm a self-taught developer that graduated in May 2022 with a bachelor's in chemical engineering. I decided to become a developer because I like problem-solving and because the versatile nature of coding appealed to me - professionally and casually.

The motivation behind this web app came to me during my first job search when a company asked to see a web application that I had made. Having none under my belt, I figured it was as good a time as any to create my first one. I racked my brain for an idea, and ended up staring at the Excel spreadsheet of hundreds of applications in front of me. As a huge fan of the subreddit r/dataisbeautiful, it didn't take me too long to come up with a satisfactory plan. Hopefully, this project turns out the way I envisioned it in the beginning and can properly showcase a bit of what I can do.
